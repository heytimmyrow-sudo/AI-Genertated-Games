Expandable Adventure Framework

Relicbound Vale

A modular top-down action-adventure foundation with a giant region, biome zones, a real village hub, gathering, crafting, camps, NPCs, shrines, combat, and progression hooks ready for expansion.

Framework Notes

Explore one giant region packed with biome pockets, Lantern Rest Village, gatherable trees and stone, a craft bench, NPCs, a merchant, sanctuaries, a dungeon entrance, relic shards, enemy camps, a mini-boss roadblock, and multiple shrine upgrade points. The codebase is still organized for future maps, quests, bosses, inventory, and save systems.

Controls

Move: WASD / Arrows / Left Stick / D-Pad Attack: Space / J / A Harvest: Attack trees and stone nodes Talk / Interact: E / X Map: M / Y Shop: 1 / 2 while trading Crafting: 1 / 2 / 3 at Oren's Bench Fast Travel: 1-4 at active sanctuaries Upgrade Shrine: 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 or D-Pad at shrine Pause: P / Start Menu: Esc Restart: R / Back

Goal

Cross the Sunken Wilds, visit Lantern Rest Village, gather wood and stone, craft at Oren's Bench, use sanctuaries as checkpoints and fast-travel points, trade with Nilo, clear the named camps, enter the Glass Vault, defeat the Stone Warden, and recover every relic shard.
