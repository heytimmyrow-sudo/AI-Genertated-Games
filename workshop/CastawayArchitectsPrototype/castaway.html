Restoration Colony Builder

Castaway Architects - Beacon Colony

Move with W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys, hold Shift to sprint.
Scroll the mouse wheel to zoom in or out. The camera now keeps a steadier angled island view.
Stand near a tree or rock and press E to harvest supplies for the stranded crew.
Press B to buy a Wood Processor. Stand near it and press F to turn Logs -> Planks.
Press C to buy a Stone Cutter. Stand near it and press R to turn Stone -> Bricks.
Press N to buy a Construction Station. Stand near it and press G to build Houses and shelter survivors.
Press J near the Construction Station to build a Bridge to the second island.
Extra colony upgrades: H - Beacon Tower | K - Wind Spire | L - Crystal Garden

Build Menu

  • B - Wood Processor ($50)
  • C - Stone Cutter ($60 + 4 Stone)
  • N - Construction Station ($80 + 4 Planks + 3 Bricks)
  • G - Survivor House (2 Planks + 1 Brick + $15)
  • J - Bridge to Second Island (40 Planks + 20 Bricks + $100)

Island Upgrades (near Construction Station)

  • H - Beacon Tower (20 Planks + 15 Bricks + $70) - trade income and rescue hope
  • K - Wind Spire (15 Planks + 10 Bricks + $40) - movement speed and colony morale
  • L - Crystal Garden (12 Planks + 12 Bricks + $50) - faster regrowth and food support

Objectives

Restore the beacon, keep the settlement fed, and give the stranded crew a reason to stay hopeful.

Gather some logs and stone to get started.
